<h1>Saathi AI</h1>
<hr>
A simple ai companion that talks to you like a usual friend there is no paywalls, no signups, no barriers, nothing just open and talk.
<hr>
<h3>What does Saathi really mean?</h3>
<p>Saathi (साथी) means "friend" in Nepali and this Saathi AI tries to depict how a usual Nepali saathi speaks to his friend simple and real with the help of ai.</p>
<hr>
<h3>Features:</h3>
<ol>
    <li>Talks like a real friend not like a corporate ai or like  a teacher scolding you </li>
    <li>No signup no paywall no nothing just open and use</li>
    <li>Speaks in a casual Nepali friend kind of tone</li>
    <li>Free forever and open source</li>
</ol>
<hr>
<h3>Tech Stack :</h3>
<ol>
    <li>Frontend : Html, Css, Javascript</li>
    <li>Backend : Node js, Vercel Serverless Functions</li>
    <li>AI Used: Hackclub Free Api</li>
</ol>
